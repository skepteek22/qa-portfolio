# [BUG-004] UI: Inconsistent alignment in Left Navigation Sidebar

**Status:** 🔴 Open  
**Project:** DemoQA  

---

## 📝 Description
Elements in the left sidebar have inconsistent padding. "Alerts, Frame & Windows" text is shifted upwards and overlaps boundaries compared to other segments.

## 🐾 Steps to Reproduce
1. Open any category on DemoQA to reveal the sidebar.
2. Compare the "Alerts, Frame & Windows" item with "Widgets".

## ❌ Actual Result
Text and icons do not align vertically; padding is inconsistent.

## ✅ Expected Result
All sidebar items must have identical font sizes, uniform padding, and centered text relative to icons.
